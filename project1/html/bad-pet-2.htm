Chameleons

Chameleons also make terrible pets to have. They are lizards, so very scaly and not soft or furry to touch, they have very weird eyes that roate 360 degrees, so you can't sneak up on them, and because they can camouflage, if they don't want you to, you can't even see them.
Trust me, you don't want a chameleon as a pet.
