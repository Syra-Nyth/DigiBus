Hello, and Welcome to the Robotics Club!

The Robotics Clun at Rangi Ruru offers many opportunities for students to learn more about robotics, and how robotics interact and tie-in with the world around them. It is open to everyone, and is based in the Creative Hub classroom.
The Robotics Club is run by Mr. Hullen, who also teaches Digital Technologies.
